About Us

Welcome to Muscle Monitor At Muscle Monitor, we are revolutionizing the way you exercise by integrating cutting-edge Internet of Things (IoT) technology to provide real-time feedback on muscle activity and posture. Our focus is on enhancing the safety and efficiency of your workouts, specifically targeting the bicep and tricep muscles.

The Problem We Solve: Maintaining proper posture and maximizing muscle activation during exercises can be challenging without professional guidance. Incorrect techniques not only reduce workout efficiency but also increase the risk of injury. Muscle Monitor addresses these issues by providing instant, data-driven feedback on your muscle activity and posture, ensuring you get the most out of every workout session.

How it Works: Our system uses surface electromyography sensors attached to your biceps and triceps. These sensors measure the electrical activity of your muscles, providing real-time data on their engagement during exercises.

Our Mission: Our mission is to empower fitness enthusiasts and professionals with precise, actionable insights into their workout routines. By leveraging advanced EMG sensors and intuitive web applications, we aim to help you achieve your fitness goals safely and effectively, whether you're working out at home or in the gym.

Our Vision: We envision a future where every fitness enthusiast, regardless of their location, can access advanced technology to enhance their workout routines. By providing accurate, real-time feedback, we aim to minimize injuries and maximize the effectiveness of every exercise, helping you achieve your fitness goals efficiently and safely.
